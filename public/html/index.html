Invisible Image Watermark Embedding

  • Welcome to our journey into the realm of invisible image watermarking! In this exploration, Group 10 will delve into the intricacies of embedding imperceptible watermarks using the Discrete Cosine Transform (DCT). Our focus lies on seamlessly integrating watermarks into the least significant bit (LSB) of DCT coefficients through a linear modulation algorithm. Join us as we navigate the balance between robustness and imperceptibility, exploring potential hardware implementations for enhanced speed and efficiency.
